Steps to Fold a Paper Airplane

  1. Take a letter-sized paper.
  2. Fold it in half lengthwise, then unfold it.
  3. Fold the top corners down to the center crease.
  4. Fold the triangle down created by the corners.
  5. Fold the wings down on both sides.
  6. Your paper airplane is ready to fly!
